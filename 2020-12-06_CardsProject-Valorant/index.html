G2 Esports

G2 Esports (formerly known as Gamers2) is a Spanish professional esports organization currently located in Berlin, Germany and founded in November 2013 by Carlos "ocelote" Rodriguez. The organization also hosts squads in League of Legends, Counter-Strike, Hearthstone, Rocket League and Rainbow Six.

Team Liquid

Team Liquid is a professional esports organization founded in the Netherlands in 2000. Originally a Brood War clan, the team switched to StarCraft II during the SC2 Beta in 2010, and became one of the most successful foreign teams. On August 7, 2020, Team Liquid entered VALORANT by signing fish123.

FunPlus Phoenix

FunPlus Phoenix (also known as FunPlus X or FPX) is an esports organization owned by Chinese game developer FunPlus. They are most well-known for their League of Legends division, which entered the LPL ahead of the 2018 season and won a World Championship in just their second year. FunPlus Phoenix now features teams in League of Legends, Fortnite, Call of Duty: Mobile and VALORANT.
